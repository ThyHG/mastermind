Mastermind

The rules are simple:

  1. The objective is to guess the randomly generated color sequence within 10 turns
  2. Click commit to submit a guess
  3. A white circle means one of your colors is correct, but in the wrong position
  4. A black circle means one of your colors is correct and also in the correct spot
  5. A colour can be in the sequence multiple times!
